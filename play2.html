HELICANUS

We have no reason to desire it, Commended to our master, not to us: Yet, ere you shall depart, this we desire, As friends to Antioch, we may feast in Tyre. Exeuntt

THALIARD

[Aside] How! the king gone!

HELICANUS

We'll mingle our bloods together in the earth, From whence we had our being and our birth.
